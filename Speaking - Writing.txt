SPEAKING TOPICS
Topic 1: Daily routine
1. What time do you usually wake up?
2. What do you eat for breakfast?
3. How do you go to school?
4. Do you exercise every day? Why/Why not?
5. When do you go to bed?
Topic 2: Family
1. How many people are in your family?
2. What do your parents do for a living?
3. Do you have any siblings? If yes, how many sisters/brothers?
4. Do you live with your grandparents?
5. What activities do you usually enjoy doing with your family?
Topic 3: Interest
1. What are your hobbies?
2. How often do you do your hobby?
3. Do you share any hobbies with your friends? Why/Why not?
4. Are you learning any new skills right now? Why/Why not?
5. What do you enjoy doing in your free time?
Topic 4: Study
1. Where do you study?
2. What do you like about your studies?
3. What is the most difficult part of your studies?
4. Do you prefer studying alone or in a team?
5. What is your favorite subject at school?
Topic 5: Travel
1. Do you enjoy traveling? Why?
2. What type of vacation do you prefer?
3. Are you planning any trips right now?
4. Do you like to travel alone or with others? Why?
5. What is your favorite travel destination?

1. Write a paragraph about yourself.

2. Write a paragraph about your daily routine.

3. Write a paragraph about your family.

4. Write a paragraph about your school.

5. Write a paragraph about your best friend.
